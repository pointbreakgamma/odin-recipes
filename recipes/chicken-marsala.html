Chicken Marsala

Description

Chicken marsala, a dish made for the heart. This dish is pan-fried with marsala wine, cream, mushrooms, and green onions. Commonly served with pasta or occasionally mashed potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 4 skinless boneless chicken breasts, pounded
  • 1 cup sliced fresh mushrooms
  • ¼ cup chopped green onion
  • 1⁄3 cup dry Marsala wine
  • salt and pepper (to taste)
  • 1⁄3 heavy cream
  • 1⁄8 cup of milk
  • fresh parsley
  • lemon wedges
