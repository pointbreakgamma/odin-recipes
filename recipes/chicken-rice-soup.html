Chicken and Rice Soup

Description

When you are so sick you can barely speak, when your bones ache, when you don't have the strength to go on, when you belly grumbles and your heat aches there is only one miracle food that can make even the most broken of hearts feel whole again: Chicken and Rice Soup.

Ingredients

  • 10 cups chicken broth
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 cup sliced celery
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • 1⁄4cup sniped parsley
  • ½ teaspoon cracked black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon dried thyme leaves
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 3⁄4pound chicken, cut into cubes
  • 2 cups of cooked riced
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • lime for garnish

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients
  2. Combine chicken broth, onion, celery, carrots, parsley, pepper thyme, and bay leaf in a dutch oven; bring to boil. Reduce heat to low; simmer until onioni and celery begin to soften
  3. Stir in chicken; simmer until chicken is no longer pink in middle. Discard bay leaf
  4. Stir in rice and lime juice; cook and stir until rice is heated through and grains separate.
  5. Garnish with lime slices
