Tofu Lo-Mein

Description

Lo-Mein noodles, top-tier noodle. Beats spaghetti in hand to hand combat on the daily. I would personally would kill for good lo-mein. Its got veggie, its got pasta, its got protein (if tofu counts).

Ingredients

  • lo mein noodles
  • 2 ½ tablespoons
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • ½ cup soy sauce
  • ½ cup teriyaki sauce
  • ½ brown sugar
  • 1 stalk green onion
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds (or to taste)
